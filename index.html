Jingbo Xu  徐静波

PhD in Informatics, supervised by Prof. Wenfei Fan
Staff engineer, Alibaba group

Office: JinHui Plaza, Beijing, China.
Contact: xujingbo.xjb at alibaba-inc.com

I am a staff engineer at the Data Computing Data Analytics and Intelligence Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy since Nov. 2018. I also worked as a joint postdoctoral researcher in Alibaba and Peking University, co-supervised by Dr. Jingren Zhou and Prof. Wenxin Li in 2019-2021. Before joining Alibaba, I was a co-founder and the CTO of 7Bridges Inc. I obtained my PhD degree from the University of Edinburgh in 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Wenfei Fan. I received my Master degree from Beihang University. My research interests include graph computing and graph data processing. Recently, I am working on the project GraphScope, a one-stop large-scale graph computing system.
